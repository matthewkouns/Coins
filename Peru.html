8 Reales
Peru - Republic
1836

Composition: Silver .903
Weight: 26.39g
Diameter: 38.5mm
Thickness: 2.6mm
Mint: Lima

Obverse: Coat of arms with llama and cornucopia, date below. Republic of Peru - Mint mark - 8R denomination
Reverse: Athena or Lady Liberty with Libertad shield and spear with Phrygian cap. Firme Y Feliz Por La Union (Strength / Firmness and happiness for the union)
